常见问题 FAQ

通常需要：有效身份证件（护照或驾照）、收入证明（工资单或银行流水，通常需要月收入3倍于月租）、信用报告授权、过往租赁记录（如有）。David 会提前为您准备个性化的申请材料清单。
Typically required: valid ID, proof of income (usually 3x monthly rent), credit check authorization, and rental history. David will prepare a personalized checklist for you.
许多华人租客（尤其是新来美国的留学生、工签人士）信用记录较短。David 熟悉这种情况，可以帮您通过提供额外的财务证明（如银行存款证明、多个月份预付等）来提高申请成功率，并寻找对此类情况更友好的房东。
David is experienced with Chinese tenants who have limited U.S. credit history, and can help you prepare alternative documentation like bank statements or prepaid rent to improve your application.
在加州，租客端通常不需要支付经纪佣金，佣金由房东支付。具体情况因每笔交易而异，David 会提前告知您任何费用安排，完全透明。
In California, tenant representation is typically free — the commission is paid by the landlord. David will clearly communicate any fee arrangements upfront.
取决于您的优先级：如果注重学区，推荐 Irvine（IUSD）或 Arcadia；如果希望靠近 LA 核心工作生活圈，推荐 Los Angeles 或 Pasadena；如果注重华人生活配套，推荐 San Gabriel、Rowland Heights 或 Arcadia；如果预算有限，Riverside 或 Chino Hills 性价比更高。David 会根据您的具体需求推荐最适合的城市。
It depends on your priorities. Irvine and Arcadia for top schools; Los Angeles and Pasadena for access to the LA core; San Gabriel and Rowland Heights for Chinese community amenities; Riverside for better value. David will recommend based on your specific needs.
部分房东接受宠物，通常需要额外的宠物押金（通常为一个月租金）或宠物租金。David 可以在找房时帮您筛选接受宠物的房源，并提前告知相关条款。
Some landlords accept pets, usually requiring additional pet deposit or pet rent. David can filter pet-friendly listings and clarify pet terms upfront.
加州标准租约通常为一年（12个月），但部分房东也接受短期租约（6个月或更短）。短期租约通常租金较高。David 会在看房时了解房东的灵活度，帮您找到符合您需求的房源。
Standard California leases are typically one year, but some landlords accept shorter terms (6 months or less). Shorter leases usually command higher rents. David will check with landlords about flexibility to find options that suit your timeline.
租客保险（Renters Insurance）通常非常便宜（每月$15-$20），但非常重要。它可以保护您的个人财产在火灾、盗窃等情况下获得赔偿，许多房东现在要求租客提供租客保险证明。David 可以为您推荐可靠的保险公司。
Renters insurance is usually inexpensive (~$15-20/month) but important — it protects your personal belongings and is now required by many landlords. David can recommend reliable providers.
