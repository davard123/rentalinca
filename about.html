我的故事 / My Story

我是 David Dai，常驻尔湾（Irvine），服务整个南加州华人社区。

作为一名在美国深耕多年的华人地产经纪人，我深知每一个华人家庭在异乡置业时所面临的挑战——语言的障碍、法规的陌生、市场的不透明。这些，我都经历过，也都克服过。

我专注于南加州的出租市场，为华人房东提供从定价、推广到租客审查的全套出租服务；同时，我也帮助有需要的家庭找到理想的租住房，以及协助买卖房产。无论您处于哪个阶段，我都希望成为您最信任的房产伙伴。

I am David Dai, based in Irvine and serving the Chinese community throughout Southern California. As a real estate professional deeply rooted in the Chinese-American community, I understand the unique challenges families face — language barriers, unfamiliar regulations, and a lack of transparent information. I specialize in Southern California's rental market, providing landlords full-service support from pricing to tenant placement, while also helping families find quality rentals and assisting buyers and sellers. Whatever stage you are at, I am here to be your most trusted real estate partner.
